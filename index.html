About Me

About Me

As a Front-End Developer, I've been giving wings to creativity putting into practice the learnings in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Git, Linux, and following towards the React universe.

I was always immersed in technology, at 12 years old I created my first websites and, during adolescence, cleaning, formatting computers, and also simple Photoshop freelances, guaranteed a good income to spend in the school canteen.

When choosing the university, the desire to live abroad and my passion for the English language led me to the University of Arts - Literature and Language-, at the UTFPR-PR.

During the course, I had the opportunity to participate in several projects, councils, organizations, commissions, and collegiate, opportunities that allowed me to realize and encourage my potential even more. Above all, the extracurricular projects helped me to develop my characteristics of determination, leadership skills, ability to work in a team and vision.
